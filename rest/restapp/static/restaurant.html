About Us

McDonald's is an American multinational fast-food corporation, founded by brothers Richard and Maurice McDonald in 1940, that grew into one of the world's largest and most recognized chains through a franchise system pioneered by Ray Kroc. With over 40,000 locations in more than 100 countries, it is known for serving a menu of hamburgers, fries, breakfast items, and other foods. The company operates through a network of independent franchisees who own and operate most locations, while McDonald's Corporation owns the real estate and provides branding and operational support
